What it does

🎯Live reticleThe crosshair samples whatever's in the middle of your viewfinder and names the color in real time.
🖼️Photo modeLoad any image from Photos or Files and drag a loupe to identify the color at a specific pixel.
🧠Smart naming31 human color names, picked in CIE Lab space — dark navy reads as Navy, not Black.
🔒Private by designThe camera feed never leaves your phone. No account, no cloud, no tracking of what you point at.

Who it's for

Roughly 1 in 12 men and 1 in 200 women have some form of color vision deficiency. The two confusions that affect almost all of them are red/green and blue/purple. I Can See!'s palette is deliberately tuned to separate those pairs, so you get an answer that's actually useful — not just "reddish".
